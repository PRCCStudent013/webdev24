Welcome to the Web Security page! In today's digital world, keeping your online activities safe is really important. Web security is all about using different methods and tools to protect your personal information, like your name and passwords, and your online stuff, like photos and messages. It helps stop bad people from getting into your accounts or sending you harmful things. Learning about web security and following good practices can help you stay safe from things like fake emails, computer viruses, and people trying to steal your identity or money. This page has lots of useful tips and advice to help you understand web security better and stay safe when you're online. Whether you're just browsing the internet or making websites yourself, knowing about web security is super important for staying safe and keeping your stuff secure.

Best Practices for Web Security:

  • How to Secure Your Computer (FTC) - Tips from the Federal Trade Commission on securing your computer against various threats.
  • Cyber Security Good Practice (NCSC) - Recommendations from the UK National Cyber Security Centre for implementing good cyber security practices.
  • Stay Safe Online (US-CERT) - Tips and resources from the United States Computer Emergency Readiness Team for staying safe online.
